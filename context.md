# CampaignOS - Product Specification & Build Context

## Vision

CampaignOS is an AI-native Campaign Operating System that sits on top of existing CRM platforms and helps marketers move from business goals to executed campaigns.

Existing CRMs are excellent systems of record. They store customers, orders, segments, and campaign history. However, marketers still spend significant time figuring out:

* Which customers should be targeted?
* What opportunity exists within the customer base?
* Which channel should be used?
* What message should be sent?
* How should campaign performance be measured?

CampaignOS acts as an AI-powered decision and execution layer that transforms business goals into campaign outcomes through a collaborative system of autonomous agents.

The goal is not to replace CRMs.

The goal is to become the AI Brain that sits above them.

Examples of future integrations:

* Salesforce
* HubSpot
* Zoho CRM
* Xeno
* Shopify
* Klaviyo

CampaignOS should be designed as a SaaS platform that can plug into existing customer engagement ecosystems.

---

# Problem Statement

Modern marketers spend too much time translating business objectives into campaign execution.

Current workflow:

Business Goal
→ Create Segment
→ Analyze Customers
→ Decide Strategy
→ Choose Channel
→ Write Content
→ Launch Campaign
→ Analyze Results

CampaignOS transforms this into:

Business Goal
→ AI Agents
→ Audience
→ Strategy
→ Content
→ Campaign Execution
→ Analytics

The marketer provides intent.

CampaignOS provides execution.

---

# Product Principles

1. AI First

The user should interact primarily through goals and natural language rather than filters and manual workflows.

2. CRM Agnostic

CampaignOS should work with any CRM system.

3. Explainability

Every recommendation generated by agents must explain why it was made.

4. Actionability

Every insight must lead to a campaign action.

5. Outcome Driven

The system should focus on revenue, retention, engagement, and customer lifetime value rather than vanity metrics.

---

# Core User Flow

The marketer lands on CampaignOS.

Instead of creating segments manually, they provide a business objective.

Example:

"Increase repeat purchases among footwear buyers who have not purchased in the last 45 days."

CampaignOS automatically:

* analyzes customer data
* discovers opportunities
* identifies audience
* generates strategy
* selects channel
* generates campaign content
* simulates execution
* tracks performance

The user can review and launch the campaign.

# Agent Architecture

CampaignOS is powered by specialized agents.

Each agent has a single responsibility.

Agents should be independent and composable.

The orchestrator coordinates them.

---

## Opportunity Agent

Purpose:

Discover business opportunities hidden within customer behavior.

Examples:

* dormant customers
* churn risks
* VIP customers
* category drop-offs
* high-value buyers
* reactivation opportunities

Output:

Opportunity report.

Example:

"There are 324 footwear customers who have not purchased in the last 45 days. Estimated recovery opportunity is ₹2.4L."

---

## Segmentation Agent

Purpose:

Convert opportunities into targetable audiences.

Examples:

* Dormant Customers
* High Value Customers
* Frequent Buyers
* At Risk Customers

Output:

Audience definitions.

The system should explain:

Why these customers were selected.

---

## Strategy Agent

Purpose:

Determine campaign strategy.

Responsible for:

* channel selection
* timing
* offer recommendation
* campaign structure

Example:

Recommended Channel:
WhatsApp

Reason:
Historically highest engagement rate for similar audiences.

Recommended Offer:
15% discount.

Reason:
Customers are price-sensitive and show declining engagement.

---

## Content Agent

Purpose:

Generate personalized communications.

Examples:

* WhatsApp messages
* Email campaigns
* Push notifications

Content should align with:

* audience profile
* campaign goal
* selected strategy

Output should include reasoning.

---

# Agent Orchestrator

The orchestrator is responsible for coordinating all agents.

Flow:

Goal Input

↓

Opportunity Agent

↓

Segmentation Agent

↓

Strategy Agent

↓

Content Agent

↓

Campaign Draft

↓

Campaign Launch

Each agent consumes outputs from previous agents.

All outputs should be structured and traceable.

# Campaign Workspace

Every generated campaign should contain:

Generated Opportunity

Selected Audience

Strategy

Recommended Channel

Generated Content

Campaign Status

# Channel Service

CampaignOS should include a separate communication service.

This service simulates real communication infrastructure.

Responsibilities:

* receive campaign requests
* process message delivery
* generate delivery events
* send callbacks

Events:

SENT

DELIVERED

READ

CLICKED

CONVERTED

This service should be independent from the core application.

Read

Top Performing Channel
